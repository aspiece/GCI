Learning Objective

Set up a professional LinkedIn profile, understand why LinkedIn matters for career opportunities, and submit your profile link as proof of completion.

Progress: 0% complete.

Activation: Get Ready to Build Your Brand

Answer these quick questions to warm up before creating your profile.

1. What is one job or career area you might want to explore after graduation?

2. Which part of your experience are you most excited to share on LinkedIn?

3. What do you think LinkedIn helps with most?

Demonstration: Follow Along Video
