Ways to Connect

Genesee Learning Lab is built on community partnerships. We welcome schools, families, industry partners, and community organizations who want to support students and expand what's possible in technology education.

🏫
Schools & Districts Interested in bringing Genesee Learning Lab programming to your school? Let's talk about partnership opportunities, including hosting students for Work-Based Learning experiences such as field trips, job shadowing, internships, and apprenticeships.
💼
Industry Partners Offer guest presentations, mentorship, internship pipelines, or project challenges for students.
🤝
Community Organizations Collaborate on community-connected projects, events, or programming that extend learning beyond the classroom.
👨‍👩‍👧
Families Have questions about student work, events, or ways to support learning at home? We'd love to hear from you.
