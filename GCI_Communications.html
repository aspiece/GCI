📖 Wednesday – Story Sprint

Tell a story to a partner in 60 seconds, then retell it in 30 seconds. Focus on clarity and key details.

Example Story: One time, I accidentally sent a text meant for my friend to my teacher. It said, “Can’t wait to talk about our secret plan!” My teacher replied, “Should I be worried?” I had to quickly explain it was about planning a surprise birthday for a classmate. Everyone laughed about it later, but it reminded me how quickly communication can go wrong if we aren’t careful.

💬 Thursday – Say It Another Way

Work as a group to rewrite the following phrases on the whiteboard to make them more positive or collaborative. Each person in the group will share, and the class will discuss the best versions together.

  • “You never listen to me.”
  • “This idea won’t work.”
  • “I already know how to do this.”
  • “That’s not my job.”
  • “This is too hard.”

⏱️ 5-Minute Timer

5:00

🪞 Thursday Reflection – Say It Another Way

Reflect on how rephrasing changed the tone of your sentences. After two minutes, your group should be ready to share how your changes improved communication.
